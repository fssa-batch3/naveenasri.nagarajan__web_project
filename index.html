6 steps for our Interior design

01

Client Meeting

Scheduling a client meeting is a vital part of the initial collaboration in finding out what clients truly expect based on their needs, taste, and style. This helps in understanding the client's vision and aspirations.

02

Innovative Design

Design is all about creativity. Interior design's expertise enables creating designs that meet a client's vision - within the budget

03

Creative Visualization

Interior design utilizes 3D rendering software to visualize a project enabling clients to understand how it will look. This further helps in making modifications besides expediting the decision-making process saving a lot of time in project completion.

04

Perfect Execution

With careful attention to space, style and function Interior design crafts interiors that are unique and reflective of the client's aspirations besides striving to explore creative opportunities within each project undertaken.

05

On-Site Supervision

Interior design offers professional on-site supervision to ensure that the project is executed with a high level of quality and matches all agreed plans, drawings and designs. Also clients are updated with the progress on a regular basis.

06

Quality

Selecting the right materials is accorded priority and care is taken to select quality materials used. The emphasis is on meeting a wide range of strict quality regulations and standard besides focussing on excellence in outcomes.

What is the best material for kitchen cabinets?

expand_more

The best material for a modular kitchen is BWR(boiling water resistant) / BWP (boiling water proof) ply. The other recommended materials include particle board and high-density-high-moisture resistant board, commonly known as HDHMR. These are core materials for carcass/internal boxes.
