Tips for advancing your career as an architect

Once you have the qualifications to become an architect, you can take steps to advance in your career, gain leadership opportunities and increase your earning potential. Use these tips to guide your career advancement as an architect:

Invest your time:

Once you have the education, gaining work experience is the best way to build your career.

Take initiative:

To advance your architect career, demonstrate leadership potential, take initiative on projects and be vocal about your ideas and suggestions.

Complete specialty courses:

Signing up for architecture courses to build your knowledge in a niche area can help you qualify for advanced architecture positions.

Earn certifications:

To complement your required licensure, earn certifications related to architecture, design, safety and construction to make your resume stand out compared to other experienced architects.
